Proposed Methodology

To analyse the data we will use both bar charts and contour maps. Bar charts will be used to determine variances between categories (e.g. genres, film actors, ratings vs revenue). Contour maps will allow us to visualise whether there is a concentration of films at a particular film rating/ box-office revenue. Scatter graphs may also be used to determine an overall trend. We will use statistical tests (e.g. Pearson’s/ Spearman’s Rank etc.) to determine the significance of any relationship found.

A successful project will reveal whether film ratings (public opinion) is indicative of the actions the public will follow (i.e. will the public/critics give a film a bad rating but still go and see it regardless?). Are film ratings ignored? And does this mean there are other factors which influence an individual’s decision to watch a film? If this is the case, a successful project will then call into question the value of rating products/ services as a whole, especially if they do not appear to influence consumer purchase trends.
